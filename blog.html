Our Qualifications & Responsibility

Our Certificates, Qualifications, & Licenses

At Media Wine & Spirits #2, we take our commitment to responsible alcohol retailing very seriously. We believe that ensuring the safe and responsible sale of alcoholic beverages is not just a legal obligation but a moral one, and it's a responsibility that extends to every member of our team.


To uphold the highest standards of safety and legality, every individual working at our liquor store undergoes comprehensive certification and training. This training equips our staff with the knowledge and skills necessary to navigate the complexities of alcohol regulations, promote responsible consumption, and prevent the sale of alcohol to minors or individuals who are already intoxicated.


Our team members are certified in accordance with local and national regulations, and they regularly update their knowledge to stay current with any changes in the industry. We understand that the sale of alcohol carries significant social and health responsibilities, and we are committed to playing our part in promoting safe and responsible drinking habits within our community.
